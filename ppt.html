What do we do?

echomods

What is it?

The principles of the echOmods is to enable a full chain of ultrasound image processing and hardware control. Four basic modules are considered. Each of this module will be landing on a motherboard, more or less a stripboard.

Key interfaces, signals and alims will be available on each of the 19 tracks on the motherboard, and will enable communication between modules. However, some tracks can be unused by the modules. These modules can have a form factor close to the arduino trinket pro, with a row of 19 pins on the side.

Modules

  • Goblin: the analog processing module, was drafted as well during Murgen.
  • Tobo: the HV and pulser module, based on a drafted close to Murgen.
  • Cletus: the piezo and servo module. Did not change.
  • One-Eye is the MicroControler module, to be working with Arduinos at first, such as the Trinket Pro.
  • Mogaba is the Alimentation Module.
  • and ... the motherboard.

Other modules, such as Silent (analog processing testing module) and Croaker (advanced microcontroler module), are in the teaser stage... More to come later on =)
